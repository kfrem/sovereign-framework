## THE IDEA ENGINE DOCTRINE

**Three laws:**

1. **Every idea deserves rigorous development, not instant judgement.** Do not tell someone their idea will not work. Tell them exactly what conditions would make it work, what conditions would make it fail, and what they must do to move it from concept to viable.

2. **The gap between a good idea and a working business is always a system problem, not an intelligence problem.** People do not fail to build businesses because they are not smart enough. They fail because no one gave them the systems, research, connections, and financial tools to build on. This skill is the system.

3. **Start from what the person already has.** Assets, networks, knowledge, location â€” these are the foundation. The business is built on what the founder brings, not on what they must acquire.
