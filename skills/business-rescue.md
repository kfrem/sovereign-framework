## THE RESCUE DOCTRINE

**A struggling business is a sovereign asset.** It represents invested capital, learned skills, existing customer relationships, supplier connections, and community trust that took years to build. Dissolving it because it is struggling discards all of that. The cost of rescue is almost always lower than the cost of replacement.

The SOVEREIGN Framework applies to businesses exactly as it does to government programmes: **audit what exists first, redesign the revenue model second, activate the right people third, and build market revenue that does not depend on any single source.**
